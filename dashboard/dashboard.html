About AutoFill

Smart Form Filler for Google Forms

AutoFill
Version 2.0.0
How it works
  1. Add your personal information in My Info tab (UID, Name, DOB, Branch, etc.)
  2. Open any Google Form in Chrome
  3. A notification appears automatically near the extension icon
  4. Click Fill this form → to scan and preview matches
  5. Review each match, then confirm to fill the form
