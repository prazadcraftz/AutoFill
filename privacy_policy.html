Privacy Policy

AutoFill — Smart Form Filler  ·  Last updated: June 2025

Summary: AutoFill stores all your data exclusively on your own device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local. Nothing is ever sent to any server, third party, or external service.

1. What data we store

  • My Info fields — The labels and values you enter (e.g. name, email, phone). Stored in chrome.storage.local as autofill_fields.
  • Brain data — A learning index of form-label aliases and confidence scores that the extension builds automatically as you use it. Stored as af_concepts, af_aliases, and af_confidence.
  • Settings — Your preferences (fill threshold, skip preview, etc.). Stored as autofill_settings.

2. Where data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device using the chrome.storage.local API, which is sandboxed to this extension only. No other website, extension, or application can access it.

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not transmit any data to any server.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting services.
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms.
  • We do not access any Google Account or Google Forms account data.
  • We do not read the content of any website other than Google Forms pages you explicitly open.

4. Permissions explained

  • storage — To save your My Info fields and Brain data locally on your device.
  • activeTab — To detect when you are on a Google Forms page and activate the fill button.
  • scripting — To inject the fill action into the active Google Forms tab when you click Fill.
  • tabs — To communicate between the popup and the active form tab after a fill is complete.
  • host_permissions (docs.google.com/forms/*) — To run the content script exclusively on Google Forms pages.

5. Data export and deletion

You can export all Brain data as a JSON file from the Dashboard → Brain tab at any time. You can delete all stored data by clicking Reset Brain in the Brain tab, or by removing the extension entirely from Chrome, which automatically clears all chrome.storage.local data.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included with the next extension update published to the Chrome Web Store. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
